# Product Information Guide

## Overview
This guide explains all the product information available in the Inventory Management System and where to find it.

## Product Fields and Their Locations

### 1. Main Products List View
The main products table displays essential information for quick scanning:

- **Product Code**: Unique identifier for the product
- **Product Name**: Descriptive name of the product
- **Category**: Product classification (e.g., Door Hardware, Accessories)
- **Material/Model**: Material type and model number
- **Brand/Grade**: Brand name and product grade
- **Quantity**: Current stock level with status indicator
- **Unit**: Measurement unit (PCS, KG, etc.)
- **Buy Price**: Purchase cost per unit
- **Sell Price**: Selling price per unit
- **Profit Margin**: Percentage profit margin
- **Actions**: View, Edit, and Delete options

### 2. Detailed Product View
Clicking the "View" icon reveals comprehensive product details:

#### Basic Information
- **Product Code**: Unique identifier
- **Product Type**: General category (e.g., Door Lock)
- **Product Name**: Specific product name
- **Model Number**: Manufacturer model reference
- **Brand**: Product brand name
- **Size**: Physical dimensions
- **Material**: Construction material
- **Color**: Product color
- **Grade**: Quality grade
- **Category**: Classification group

#### Stock & Pricing Details
- **Current Stock Level**: Quantity with visual status indicator
- **Unit**: Measurement unit
- **Unit Quantity**: Units per package (if applicable)
- **Purchase Rate**: Cost to acquire
- **Sell Rate**: Customer selling price
- **Approximate Rate**: Market reference price
- **Authentication Rate**: Verified pricing
- **Profit Margin**: Percentage gain
- **Total Stock Value**: (Quantity × Purchase Rate)

#### Additional Information
- **Created At**: Timestamp when product was added
- **Last Updated**: Timestamp of last modification

## How to Access Product Information

### 1. Quick Overview
- Navigate to the "Products" section from the sidebar
- Browse the table for essential product details
- Use search and filter options to locate specific items

### 2. Detailed Information
- Click the eye icon (View) next to any product in the table
- Review all available product attributes
- Edit product details using the "Edit Product" button

### 3. Adding New Products
- Click "Add Product" button in the Products section
- Fill in all relevant fields for complete product information
- Save to add the product to inventory

## Best Practices for Product Information Management

### 1. Complete Data Entry
- Fill in all available fields when adding products
- Use consistent naming conventions
- Include accurate measurements and specifications

### 2. Regular Updates
- Update quantities after transactions
- Adjust pricing as market conditions change
- Review and update product information periodically

### 3. Data Accuracy
- Verify product codes are unique
- Ensure pricing reflects current market rates
- Confirm stock quantities through periodic audits

## Troubleshooting Missing Information

### Common Issues
1. **Empty Fields**: Some fields may show "N/A" if not populated
2. **Incomplete Imports**: Excel imports may miss fields not in the spreadsheet
3. **Formatting Issues**: Numbers may appear as text if not properly formatted

### Solutions
1. Edit the product to add missing information
2. Re-import with complete data
3. Use the "Edit Product" form to correct formatting

## Reporting on Product Information

### Available Reports
- **Stock Report**: Shows inventory levels and status
- **Profit/Loss Report**: Displays financial performance by product
- **Purchase Report**: Tracks buying history
- **Sales Report**: Monitors selling activity

### Export Options
- Export all product data to Excel for offline analysis
- Generate detailed reports for specific categories
- Share product catalogs with stakeholders
